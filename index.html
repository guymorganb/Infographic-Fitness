Emotional Wellness

Physical activities overcome negative emotions and physiological barriers, benefits include enhanced fitness, illness prevention, and stress relief. Exercise combats the effects of aging.[2]

Mental Wellness

Aerobic fitness, consistently shows significant improvements in cognitive abilities, and aerobic fitness shares a moderate yet positive link with academic accomplishments [3]

Physical Wellness

College students view muscular fitness as an indicator of health. Maintaining physical activity, through muscle-strengthening exercises, can enhance quality of life and cardiovascular health of college students.[4]
